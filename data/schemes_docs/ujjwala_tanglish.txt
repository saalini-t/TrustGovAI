Ujjwala Yojana oru free LPG connection scheme, BPL families-ku.
Indha scheme moolam poor women-ku free gas cylinder and stove kudukum.
Ujjwala 2.0 la migrant workers-kum benefit kidaikum.

Ujjwala eligibility enna:
- BPL family-la irukanum
- Applicant woman aaga irukanum (18 years and above)
- Veetla vera LPG connection irukka koodathu

Ujjwala apply epdi panrathu:
- Nearest LPG distributor-ku ponga
- Aadhaar card, BPL card, bank passbook edunga
- Free-a connection kudupanga

Ujjwala scheme benefits:
- Free LPG connection
- First cylinder free
- Stove free
- Subsidy on refills

Ujjwala helpline: 1906
Online apply: pmuy.gov.in
